How It Works

1. Crop Recommendation and Improvement Suggestions

This tool helps you select the best crop for your soil and provides suggestions to improve crop yield. Here's how it works:

  • Fill out the form with details such as soil type, weather conditions, and other relevant factors.
  • Submit the form, and the model will analyze the data to recommend a crop that is most suitable for your conditions.
  • Additionally, you'll receive suggestions to improve crop yield based on the provided information.
Go to Crop Recommendation

2. Plant Disease Detection

This feature allows you to identify diseases affecting your plants by analyzing images. Follow these steps:

  • Take a clear picture of the affected plant's leaf or area showing symptoms.
  • Upload the image through the Disease Detection form.
  • Our model will analyze the image and display the predicted disease, helping you take appropriate action to protect your crops.
Go to Disease Detection

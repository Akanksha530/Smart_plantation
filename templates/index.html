How It Works

Crop Recommendation and Improvement Suggestions

This tool helps you select the best crop for your soil and provides personalized suggestions to improve crop yield.

  • Fill out the form with details such as soil type, location, and weather conditions.
  • Submit the form for crop recommendations tailored to your conditions.
  • Receive improvement suggestions based on your provided information.
Go to Crop Recommendation

Plant Disease Detection

Identify diseases affecting your plants by analyzing images of affected leaves or areas showing symptoms.

  • Take a clear picture of the affected area.
  • Upload the image through the Disease Detection form.
  • Our model analyzes the image and predicts the disease to guide appropriate action.

Our Mission

At Smart Plantation Monitoring System, we aim to revolutionize the agricultural landscape by leveraging modern technology. Our mission is to support farmers worldwide in optimizing yields, preserving resources, and practicing sustainable agriculture for a better future.

We believe that by making advanced technology accessible to farmers and plantation managers, we can foster a healthier planet while increasing food security and profitability for all.

Through our platform, we aim to make advanced agricultural insights and solutions accessible to all, helping communities to achieve food security, increase profitability, and contribute to a resilient agricultural sector. We believe that technology should work hand-in-hand with nature, promoting balance and efficiency.
